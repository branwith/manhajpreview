About us

Learn more about Manhajurrashad Islamic College

Manhajurrashad Islamic College is an esteemed institution of higher learning, situated in the picturesque region of Kerala, India. The college is affiliated with Darul Huda Islamic University and is committed to providing an excellent standard of Islamic education integrated with modern curriculum and moral upbringing. Our college's aim is to create a new generation of Sunni scholars who are well-versed in both Islamic and contemporary education. To achieve this goal, we have a team of experienced and qualified teachers who guide our students in their academic pursuits. Our faculty members are passionate about their subjects and use innovative teaching methodologies to make learning more interactive and engaging.

At Manhaj, we provide excellent facilities to support our students' learning and research activities. Our library is one of the largest in the region, with an extensive collection of Islamic books, journals, and publications. The computer lab is equipped with modern computing equipment and software, providing students with hands-on experience in computer-based activities. Our classrooms are equipped with Android smart TVs and audio-visual equipment, creating an engaging and dynamic learning environment for our students.

We offer a comprehensive curriculum that covers various aspects of religion, spirituality, intellectuality, socio-political, and economic dimensions. Our graduates are skilled in these areas and are well-equipped to take on the challenges of the world. Our college is committed to providing a holistic education that prepares our students for the future, and we take pride in our alumni who have excelled in various fields.

Founder

K Abdul Qadar Musliyar

Haji K Abdul Qadar Musliyar is the founder of Manhajurrashad Islamic College. He is a well-known Islamic scholar, educationist, and social worker who has dedicated his life to promoting Islamic education and values. Born and raised in Chelembra, a small village in Kerala, India, Haji K Abdul Qadar Musliyar started his career as a teacher and went on to become a renowned scholar and mentor to thousands of students.

He is known for his unique approach to teaching Islamic studies, combining traditional teachings with modern pedagogical methods. Haji K Abdul Qadar Musliyar believes that education is the key to social and economic development, and he has worked tirelessly to establish schools and colleges that provide high-quality education to students from all walks of life.

Through his vision and dedication, Haji K Abdul Qadar Musliyar has created an educational institution that is recognized for its excellence in Islamic education and academic achievement. His mission is to empower students to become leaders who will contribute to their communities and make a positive impact on the world.

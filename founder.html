Founder

K Abdul Qadar Musliyar

Haji K Abdul Qadar Musliyar is the founder of Manhajurrashad Islamic College. He is a well-known Islamic scholar, educationist, and social worker who has dedicated his life to promoting Islamic education and values. Born and raised in Chelembra, a small village in Kerala, India, Haji K Abdul Qadar Musliyar started his career as a teacher and went on to become a renowned scholar and mentor to thousands of students.

He is known for his unique approach to teaching Islamic studies, combining traditional teachings with modern pedagogical methods. Haji K Abdul Qadar Musliyar believes that education is the key to social and economic development, and he has worked tirelessly to establish schools and colleges that provide high-quality education to students from all walks of life.

Through his vision and dedication, Haji K Abdul Qadar Musliyar has created an educational institution that is recognized for its excellence in Islamic education and academic achievement. His mission is to empower students to become leaders who will contribute to their communities and make a positive impact on the world.

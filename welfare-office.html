WELFARE CO-ORDINATOR

Labeeb Hudawi

Mr. Labeeb Hudawi, the welfare coordinator and happiness minister at Manhajurrashad Islamic College, is dedicated to prioritizing student happiness and well-being. With a deep understanding of holistic development, he creates a nurturing and supportive environment where students can thrive academically, socially, and emotionally.

As the welfare coordinator, Mr. Labeeb Hudawi actively engages with students, ensuring their needs are met and their concerns are addressed. He builds strong relationships based on trust and respect, fostering open communication. Additionally, in his role as the happiness minister, he organizes activities and workshops that promote positivity, resilience, and mental well-being. By fostering a supportive network and encouraging a positive mindset, he contributes to a college community where students feel valued, supported, and motivated to achieve their goals. Mr. Labeeb Hudawi's commitment to student happiness enhances the overall educational experience at Manhajurrashad Islamic College.
